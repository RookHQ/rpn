Technical FAQ

Explore the technical details of RPN

How does RPN achieve decentralized anonymity?

RPN leverages blockchain technology, using smart contracts and oracles as relay servers. When a user calls the smart contract with a URL and request type, an oracle is used to make an external server call. The data is then encrypted with the sender's public key and emitted as an event. This ensures that user data remains anonymous and secure, as the blockchain randomly allocates nodes and oracles for each function call

What mechanisms protect my data from surveillance and tracking?

RPN employs end-to-end encryption. Your data is encrypted with your public key and can only be decrypted with your private key. This secure encryption method ensures that your data remains confidential and immune to surveillance.

How does RPN offer global access for users?

RPN's decentralized nature allows users to access the internet from anywhere in the world, even in regions with strict data regulations. By using a random node allocation mechanism and oracle selection, your online presence remains private and untraceable.

Can I configure RPN to meet my specific needs?

Yes, RPN offers versatile configuration options. You can customize your requests, including the URL, request type, and other configurations, to tailor the service to your unique requirements. This flexibility is a key feature of RPN.
